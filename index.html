    Welcome To Your New Home!

    So you still haven't decided what to cook yet!? Have you been putting off going to your local store? Say no more, Your in the right place! Dynamite Recipes is an application that helps you find recipes using ingredients that you already have!

Type your ingredients that you have available to you. After making your choices. "Add" those ingredients, hit the "Get Recipe" button and numerous recipes will be available to you in seconds! If you change your mind, you can click the trash button to delete any of the selected choices.
